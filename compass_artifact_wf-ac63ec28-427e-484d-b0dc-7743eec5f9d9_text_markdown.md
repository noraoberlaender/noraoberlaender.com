## Executive Summary

Nora Oberlaender (born 1994, Berlin) is Managing Director and Chief Digital Officer at the HÜBNER Group, a German family-owned industrial company with **3,500+ employees** worldwide. She represents the **third generation** of family leadership as the great-granddaughter of founder Kurt Hübner. Before joining HÜBNER's executive management in March 2025, she built expertise in the green hydrogen sector through roles at H2 MOBILITY Deutschland and STOFF2. She is an industrial engineer with international education spanning TU Berlin, Sweden, Brazil, and Singapore.
